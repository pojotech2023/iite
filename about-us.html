About Us

About Circuites

IITE is a leading institution dedicated to software education and project development. As the authorized training center of Kerala State Rutronix in Kottiyam, Kollam, we provide high-quality training programs designed to equip students with the skills needed to succeed in the technology industry. Our focus on project-based learning ensures that students gain practical, real-world experience alongside theoretical knowledge.

We offer a wide range of professional courses, including PGDCA, DCA, CTTC, CWPDE, Tally, SAP, and more. These programs cover essential areas such as software development, office automation, web programming, desktop publishing, computer-aided design, and financial accounting. Our curriculum is designed to align with industry standards, ensuring our students are job-ready upon completion.

Our Vision

To empower individuals with industry-relevant software skills through quality education and hands-on learning.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between education and industry by providing practical, project-based software training.

IITE, the authorized training center of Kerala State Rutronix in Kottiyam, Kollam, offers industry-focused software project training and certified courses. Join us to elevate your skills and unlock new career opportunities.
